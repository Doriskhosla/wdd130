"The Palacio de Bellas Artes is a beautiful place dedicated to the fine arts, located in historic center of Mexico City. It is known for its stunning architecture, combining Art Nouveau on the outside and Art Deco on the inside. The building is made of white marble, and its roof has a colorful dome covered with tiles that shine in the sunlight. Inside, visitors can see: Large murals paited by famous Mexican artists, and impressive theater and concert hall."/p>

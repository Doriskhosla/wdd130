    Our history

    Our mission is to provide exceptional experience rafting adventures filled with exciting, safety, and spectacular connection to nature. Come and explore the wild rivers and create a amazing memories!

    The foundation of this company started with a wild inspiration in January 2012, with a group of passionate river guides. Over the years, we have grown into one of the most trusted rafting companies, thanks to that we have the opportunity to share the most excitement experiences to all wild spirits like you around the world!
